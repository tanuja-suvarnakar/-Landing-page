Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how AcmeFlow uses cookies and similar technologies.

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. We use cookies to help our site and product function properly and to understand how they are used.

2. Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies – required for core functionality, such as authentication.
  • Analytics cookies – help us understand usage and improve performance.
  • Preference cookies – remember your settings and preferences.

3. Managing cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. If you disable certain cookies, some features of the site or product may not function correctly.

4. Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review it periodically for any changes.

5. Contact

For questions about this Cookie Policy, contact us at privacy@acmeflow.com.
