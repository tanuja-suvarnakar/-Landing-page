FAQ

Frequently asked questions about AcmeFlow.

How long does it take to implement AcmeFlow? +

Most B2B customers go live in 4–6 weeks. Our team maps your current workflows, configures automations, and trains your admins and end‑users. Complex, multi‑region deployments may take longer, but we guide you through each step.

Which CRMs and tools does AcmeFlow integrate with? +

We offer native integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, Microsoft Dynamics, Slack, Google Workspace, and major data warehouses. Our API and webhook framework make it easy to connect other B2B tools in your stack.

Is AcmeFlow secure and enterprise‑ready? +

Yes. AcmeFlow is built for modern B2B enterprises, with SSO (SAML/OIDC), role‑based access controls, audit logging, and encryption in transit and at rest. Our team can support your security review and vendor assessment process.
