Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AcmeFlow Inc. (“AcmeFlow”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects personal data in connection with our services.

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Scope

This Policy applies to personal data we process when you visit our website, create an account, or use the AcmeFlow platform in connection with your organization. It does not apply to third‑party services that integrate with AcmeFlow.

2. Information we collect

We may collect:

  • Contact details such as name, email address, and company information.
  • Account and usage information, including login details and activity in the product.
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device identifiers.

3. How we use information

We use personal data to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the AcmeFlow platform.
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Send service‑related communications and updates.
  • Analyze usage to improve performance and security.

4. Data sharing

We may share data with trusted service providers who process data on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics, support tools), subject to appropriate contractual protections. We do not sell personal data.

5. Data retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes, in accordance with our internal policies.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete certain personal data, or to object to specific processing activities. To make a request, contact us at privacy@acmeflow.com.

7. International transfers

AcmeFlow may process data globally. Where applicable, we use appropriate safeguards when transferring personal data across borders.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and update the “Last updated” date.

9. Contact us

If you have questions about this Policy or our data practices, contact us at privacy@acmeflow.com.
